Collection of legends

"Curious Myths of the Middle Ages" presents a collection of legends that explore human fears, hopes, and the supernatural. Baring-Gould recounts each tale, investigates its origins, and reveals how it reflects the values of medieval society.

Curiosity about the Supernatural

Baring-Gould combines narrative and analysis, retelling each myth and then deconstructing it with historical and theological insight. His goal was to understand the origins of these stories and their cultural impact.

Quill

Tales of distant lands, mythical beasts, and impossible journeys reflect both the fear and wonder that shaped the medieval worldview.

This is a study project for CS50x . The book is in the public domain .
